JNC

'Joventut Nacionalista de Catalunya' is the first youth political organization in the country. Founded in 1980, working for a just and noble cause, freedom of Catalonia and its people.
I collaborated on an iOS app app where you will find all the information about the organization: News, agenda, ideas and direct contact with the people who are part of. More precisely I made the backend part of the app using Ruby on Rails, an admin panel to send notifications to the users and the notifications system itself using Amazon web services and SNS.
